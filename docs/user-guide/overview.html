User Guide Overview

This guide will help you get the most out of Hive-Pal for your beekeeping management. Below is an overview of the main features and functionality.

Getting Started​

After installing Hive-Pal, you'll need to:

  1. Create an account: Register with your email and password
  2. Set up your first apiary: Create at least one apiary to get started
  3. Add hives: Add your existing hives to the system
  4. Record inspections: Begin tracking your hive inspections

Main Features​

Getting Around​

  • Apiary Management — create and manage multiple apiary locations with GPS and notes.
  • Hive Management — track individual hives, box setup, status, and history.
  • Quick Actions (FAB) — the floating button for fast inspection, scheduling, and actions.

Inspections​

  • Inspections — detailed records with subjective ratings or data-driven counts.
  • Quick Check — fast, tag-based field observations.
  • Batch Inspections — inspect many hives in one guided run.
  • Scheduled Inspections — plan ahead using the weather forecast.
  • Audio & AI Transcription — speak your notes and let AI structure them.
  • Bulk Add — apply an inspection, action, or queen to many hives at once.

Colony Management​

  • Queen Management — details, marking, movement history, and transfers.
  • Equipment — plan and track equipment against target hive counts.
  • Harvests — log and track honey harvests.
  • Weather — forecasts and conditions tied to your apiaries.

Data & Devices​

  • QR Codes — print labels that scan straight to a hive or inspection.
  • Import & Export — back up or migrate your account data.
  • Calendar — past and upcoming inspections across all apiaries.
  • Reports & Analytics — trends for health, feeding, scores, and honey.
  • Apiary Assistant — ask questions about your hives in plain language.
  • HiveScale Integration — live weight, temperature, and off-grid telemetry from HiveScale devices.

Tools & Account​

  • Beekeeping Tools — free syrup calculator, brood timeline, and swarm management.
  • Logging In — password, magic link, and passkey sign-in.
  • Account & Settings — password, files, and preferences.

Mobile Usage​

Hive-Pal is designed with mobile usage in mind, making it ideal for:

  • Recording inspections while at the apiary
  • Quickly checking hive histories in the field
  • Adding notes and observations on the go

Data Privacy​

As a self-hosted application, all your beekeeping data remains under your control. Your sensitive information never leaves your server unless you choose to export it.

Getting Help​

If you encounter issues or have questions about using Hive-Pal:

  • Check the troubleshooting section
  • Visit the GitHub repository for the latest updates
  • Report bugs or request features through GitHub issues
