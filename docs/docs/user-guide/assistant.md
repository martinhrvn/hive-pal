# Apiary Assistant

The Assistant (`/assistant`) lets you ask questions about your operation in plain language and get answers drawn from your own Hive-Pal records — useful for comparing hives and spotting outliers.

## What You Can Ask

- Compare colonies — "which hives are weakest right now?"
- Surface follow-ups — "which hives need feeding?"
- Review recent activity across an apiary without clicking through each hive.

## How It Works

The Assistant reads your apiary and hive data to answer questions. As with any AI feature, treat its answers as a helpful starting point and verify against the underlying [inspections](./inspections) before making decisions.

## FAQ

**Does it use my data?**
Yes — its answers are grounded in your apiaries, hives, and inspection records so they're relevant to your operation.

**Should I rely on it for treatment decisions?**
Use it to surface and summarize information, but confirm against your actual records and your own judgement before acting.
