# Reports & Analytics

The reports dashboard (`/reports`) turns your inspection and harvest records into trends and charts so you can see how your colonies are doing over time.

## What's Included

- **Health trends** — colony health and inspection scores across the season.
- **Feeding** — supplemental feeding over time.
- **Hive scores** — population, stores, and queen scores tracked per hive.
- **Honey production** — harvest totals (see [Harvests](./harvest)).

## Filtering

Choose the period you want to analyze — year-to-date or a custom date range — to focus on a season or compare across time.

## Exporting

Export reports to **CSV** (for spreadsheets) or **PDF** (for sharing or records).

## FAQ

**Where does the data come from?**
From your [inspections](./inspections), feeding [actions](./inspections#actions), and [harvests](./harvest). The more consistently you record, the more useful the trends.

**Can I export raw data?**
Use CSV export here for charts, or the full [account export](./import-export) for a complete data dump.
