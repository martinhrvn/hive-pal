# Calendar

The calendar (`/calendar`) gives you a single timeline of inspection activity across all your hives and apiaries — what you've done and what's coming up.

## What You'll See

- **Completed inspections** on the days they happened.
- **[Scheduled inspections](./scheduled-inspections)** on their planned dates.
- An at-a-glance view to spot gaps and plan the next visit.

## Using It

- Browse by month to review activity and upcoming work.
- Use it alongside [Scheduled Inspections](./scheduled-inspections) to plan around the weather forecast.

## FAQ

**Does the calendar show all my apiaries?**
Yes — it aggregates inspection activity across every apiary you have access to.

**Can I subscribe to it in my own calendar app?**
Hive-Pal can generate iCal feeds for calendar integration (these use a signed token). Check your settings for the feed URL.
