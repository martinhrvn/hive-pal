# Beekeeping Tools

Hive-Pal includes a set of free, open-source beekeeping tools (`/tools`) that anyone can use — **no account or login required**. They're handy references whether or not you track hives in Hive-Pal.

## Sugar Syrup Calculator

Work out exactly how much sugar and water to mix for a target volume of syrup at common ratios:

- **1:1** (spring stimulative feeding)
- **3:2**
- **2:1** (autumn / winter stores)

Results are available in metric or imperial units.

Path: `/tools/syrup-calculator`

## Brood Development Timeline

Visualize the development stages of queen, worker, and drone brood with day counts — useful for timing inspections, predicting emergence, and planning swarm-control interventions.

Path: `/tools/brood-timeline`

## Swarm Management

A step-by-step reference for swarm control, including the **Demaree method** with follow-up timing.

- Overview: `/tools/swarm-management`
- Demaree walkthrough: `/tools/swarm-management/demaree`

## FAQ

**Do I need an account to use the tools?**
No. The tools are public and work without logging in.

**Are the tools accurate for my climate?**
They're general-purpose references. Adapt timings and feeding to your local conditions and your colonies' needs.
