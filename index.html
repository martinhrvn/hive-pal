About Hive-Pal

An open-source beekeeping management application

Mobile-First Design

Take your beekeeping records to the field with our responsive mobile interface. Designed for field use on your smartphone or tablet.

Apiary Management

Manage your apiaries with location tracking and organization. Keep all your hive locations organized in one place.

Hive Tracking

Monitor individual hives, their status, and configuration. Track equipment and maintain complete colony records.

Inspection Forms

In Progress

Record observations with streamlined inspection forms. Quick toggles and rating scales for efficient data entry.

Queen Management

In Progress

Track queen details, marking colors, and replacement history. Document queen performance and lineage over time.

Weather Integration

Planned

Record weather conditions during inspections. Track environmental factors alongside colony observations.

Treatment Tracking

In Progress

Log treatments with dates and methods. Keep records of all medications and interventions.

Performance Insights

Planned

View colony strength and production trends over time. Use data to optimize your beekeeping practices.

Self-Hosted

Run Hive-Pal on your own server or local network. Keep complete control of your beekeeping data.

Project Status: Active Development

Hive-Pal is currently under active development. Some features described here are still being implemented or refined. We welcome contributors to help build and improve this open-source tool for the beekeeping community.
